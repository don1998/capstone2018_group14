How does it work?

Welcome! Are you a gaming aficionado? Or are you just a cursory gamer? Neither?

Well, we invite you to join us on our adventure, to create the 'perfect' AI enemy. On this journey, we endeavour to: explain how we have created the 'perfect' AI enemy, and how this will impact the future of gaming. Let's get started with some basic information.

What is AI?

AI stands for Artificial Intelligence. This is the notion that technological devices such as computers can be trained to think, in approximately the same way that living creatures do. One way to test this concept, is that providing a device with a set of data, it can make decisions, outside of what it was coded to do.

What exactly did Danimya Tech do?

We created a game environment using Unity that mimics a boss fight in a typical combat game. This boss-fight setting served as the proof of concept of our AI enemy who can learn to play a game given a set of inputs. about the gaming environment. This means that AI enemies no longer need to be explicitly told what to do, and what not to do, but rather, can be provided with some obsevrations about a game environment, actions that can be performed and an opponent. Based on the actions the AI agent makes it is either rewarded or punished. The agent, in time, will associate its actions with these rewards and will act in such a way as to maximise its rewards. This process is called 'training'. Because of this the enemy will then learn how to play the game. This benefits you, the player, because you will then have a more diverse and challenging gaming experience.

Results?

The above video shows our AI enemy in the early stages of training. Note that it's actions seem to be random in that clip and its overall decision making is poor. Conversely, in the second clip, our AI is now well into training and the improvements in his decision making and level of challenge being posed to the player are clear.
